Khuangpuilam Chanchin Tlangpui


Khuangpuilam hi Kolasib khaw chhung a veng chhim tawp bera awm, Aizawl atanga kal in veng hmasa ber, Kolasib khaw luh tan tirh a awm a ni. A veng boruak, hmun leh hma hi rem tak leh nuam tak a nih avang hian miten inbenbel nan an bawh nasat ber pawl Kolasib ah chuan a ni. Kum 2014 Dec khan Vengthar YMA atangin Khuangpuilam YMA hi a indang a. Khuangpuilam Village Council (VC) pawh kum 2015 atanga mahnia inrelbawl turin Vengthar atang bawkin a indang leh a ni. Tin, Khuangpuilam vengchhung ah hian sawrkar departnent pawimawh tak tak Excise & Narcotic Department, SP Office leh Soil & Water Conservation Department te an awm a ni.
